TEETH BRUSHING:

Most people want to keep their teeth and have attractive smiles. This requires frequent brushing and good dental habits. if you fail to brush your teeth they are more likely to become discolored, get cavities and possible fall out i, therefore, strongly recommend you (as well as indulging your children into the habit of brushing for parent) to brush your teeth twice daily and after every meal to prevent tooth decay.

BAD EFFECT OF NOT BRUSHING YOUR MOUTH

  • Cause cavities or tooth decay
  • Dry cracked lips
  • Unpleasant taste in the mouth
  • Decrease appetite
  • Bleeding gums

Tips on Mouth Care:

  1. Eat a well-balanced alow-sugar diet.nd
  2. Encourage high fluid intake, if the person is not under any fluid restriction, to keep his mouth fresh.
  3. Eat citrus fruits (e.g. oranges), unless this is against medical advice, as these stimulate the production of saliva which naturally cleanse the mouth.
  4. If the person's mouth is dry, and he is on restricted fluids, sugar-free chewing gum may encourage the natural flow of saliva and helps to keep his mouth moist.
  5. Brush.
